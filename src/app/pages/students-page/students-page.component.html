The ESNcard

The ESNcard is first a membership card of the Erasmus Student Network. It is a proof of membership in an ESN section and so indirectly in the ESN Network. Secondly, it is used as a discount card in many cities and countries around Europe and provides you access to all services and discounts offered by ESN and our partners to international students. The ESNcard is valid for 12 months and only valid with a photo and correct personal data.

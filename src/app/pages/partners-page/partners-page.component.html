The ESNcard is the membership card of the Erasmus Student Network! It is the proof that you belong to the Erasmus Generation - in an ESN section as well as in the network itself. It also serves as a discount card which gives you access to services and discounts all over Europe as well as online. To get an ESNcard, contact your closest ESN section. To find out more about discounts, check out our partners. You will find more information on how to register your ESNcard on ESNcard.org. You are a company or association and would like to work with a young, international student network based in Germany? Then become our partner! We are currently cooperating with several companies in very different ways. From discounts connected to our membership card, the ESNcard, sponsoring or collaborating on projects, the possibilities are endless. If you would like to contact us and discuss your ideas, feel free to send an email to our partnership manager at partnership@esn-germany.de.
ESN Germany advocates for students on international mobilities, incoming as well as outgoing, with a focus on the Erasmus+ Programme. We represent their needs and rights on the local, national and international level. Furthermore, we advocate for social inclusion and accessible international mobility programmes for every person, regardless of their background.
We are constantly looking for new institutional cooperations with organisations that have similar goals and aims. If your organisation would like to work with us, send us an email at board@esn-germany.de and we can talk about possibilities for joint projects or general cooperation opportunities!

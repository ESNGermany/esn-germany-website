The ESNcard is the membership card of the Erasmus Student Network! It is the proof that you belong to the Erasmus Generation - in an ESN section as well as in the network itself. It also serves as a discount card which gives you access to services and discounts all over Europe as well as online. To get an ESNcard, contact your closest ESN section. To find out more about discounts, check out our partners. You will find more information on how to register your ESNcard on ESNcard.org. You are a company or association and would like to work with a young, international student network based in Germany? Then become our partner! We are currently cooperating with several companies in very different ways. From discounts connected to our membership card, the ESNcard, sponsoring or collaborating on projects, the possibilities are endless. If you would like to contact us and discuss your ideas, feel free to send an email to our partnership manager at partnership@esn-germany.de.
Swapfiets:
From classic to electric, Swapfiets has them all. Swapfiets is a bike membership, where for a low, fixed monthly fee, you get a bike and they make sure it always works. Use the code „SWAPSTUDENT“ for a 19.99€ discount on your subscription.
Flink:
Groceries delivered in minutes. Your one-stop online shop, from fresh produce and household staples to cooking essentials, Flink is the service that always delivers to your door. Use the code „ESNFLINK“ for a 15€ discount on your first order (over 30€).
Eurosender:
Your Exchange is almost here and you still don’t know how you’ll bring everything to Germany? Eurosender has a solution: You can ship your luggage to your destination for an affordable price. Use the code „ESNGER20“ for a 10% discount.
Spotahome:
Find and book your ideal place for months. Spotahome is an 100% online booking platform, offering an exhaustive list of apartments, rooms, studios, and student residences. Use the code „ESNGERMAN25“ for a 25% discount on the service fee.
Wanderlust:
Wanderlust Trips make travel fun, easy and sociable! Get a 10% discount on all trips by Wanderlust using the ESNcard discount code while booking.
TK:
TK will assist you with any questions about your health insurance. They are the largest provider of public health insurance in Germany. Due to its great price/performance ratio, TK is especially popular with students from Germany and abroad. Fill in the easy online application and become a member of TK.
The Economist:
The Economist is a weekly newspaper published and printed in magazine format and digitally. It focuses on current affairs, international business, politics, technology ans culture. Click the logo for a 50% discount for the first 12 weeks of a subscription and 20% afterwards.
ESN Germany advocates for students on international mobilities, incoming as well as outgoing, with a focus on the Erasmus+ Programme. We represent their needs and rights on the local, national and international level. Furthermore, we advocate for social inclusion and accessible international mobility programmes for every person, regardless of their background.
We are constantly looking for new institutional cooperations with organisations that have similar goals and aims. If your organisation would like to work with us, send us an email at board@esn-germany.de and we can talk about possibilities for joint projects or general cooperation opportunities!

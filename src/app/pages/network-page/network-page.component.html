ESN Germany

The Erasmus Student Network Germany, a non-profit student organisation, is the consolidation of the German local Erasmus student initiatives and their volunteers. Our voluntary members, mostly students, take care of approximately 10.600 exchange students every year in about 40 sections all over Germany. In Europe, ESN is represented in more than 1000 Higher Education Institutions from 42 countries. This makes our organisation one of the largest student initiatives in Europe.

The National Board

ESN Germany is formed by the National Board. Its purpose is to support the local sections in all kinds of topics, to be an intermediate between them and ESN International, and to strengthen the German network plus the relationships between the sections and their members. Each one of the board members of ESN Germany is a volunteer and was elected at our January National Assembly by our sections.
